The event · Sunday Live

How the live works

One hour, live on YouTube, free. No slides. Tudor builds a real AI animation from scratch, start to finish, and the audience watches it happen.

0-5 min
Welcome + promise. Who it's for, what they walk away with today.
5-40 min
Build live. One real AI animation from a blank prompt to a finished, real-looking video. The exact tools, prompts and settings, on screen.
40-50 min
Q&A. Live questions, build on requests from the chat.
50-60 min
Next step. Invite into the Academy ($49/mo or $400/yr) for those who want the full system.

Post-live · Measurement

What we measure after the live

The live is the conversion event. After it, we read the numbers that tell us if the funnel converts, not just if it fills.

Show-up rate
Registrants who actually attended the live.
Watch time
Avg view duration + peak concurrent viewers.
Chat engagement
Comments and questions during the build.
WhatsApp joins
Attendees who joined the community from the live.
Skool signups
$49/mo or $400/yr conversions from live + follow-up.
Replay + email
Post-live YouTube views + follow-up email opens/clicks.

Plus a 1-question feedback survey to attendees ("what should I build next?") that feeds the next live's topic and the next ad angles.
